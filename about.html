About Me

Hi I’m Shane Adduru, a Bachelor of Science Information Technology student. I enjoy singing even if it’s just humming sometimes. I also like cooking when I’m in the mood, and I often watch movies and dramas — especially when I want to relax or take a break from school work.

I created this blog to share the things that make me happy — from my favorite song, "Everything I Own" which reminds me of my parents because they are the ones who gave me life and raised me with love and care. The song’s message reflects how I would give up everything just to show how much they mean to me. No material thing can ever compare to the sacrifices they made and the love they’ve given.

Simple recipes I’ve tried, like "Adobo", because it’s my specialty, and dramas or movies that I really enjoyed. Sometimes, I also write random thoughts about life or school.

This blog is simple, but I hope you find something here that makes you smile. Thanks.
